Overview

Hi, I'm Dom. I'm a software engineer in the Post Trade Systems team at Maven Securities, a leading market maker and proprietary trading firm.

Prior to Maven I worked as a software engineer and dev team lead at Applied Systems, and as a statistical analyst at an insurance company called First Central.

Here you can read about me, my background, and what I'm currently working on. You can also read articles I write for my blog.

Background

I started out writing C++ at university, with a subsequent period of writing Python for data science at First Central. I've long since settled on C# as the perfect balance between versatility, productivity and performance. Day to day at Maven I'm working with the .NET Framework, .NET Core, and Postgres for persistence.

Outside of work I also enjoy trying out new technologies. As an example this website is backed by Jekyll, the Liquid template language and SCSS.

Light++: my personal light theme for VSCode. Find it by searching for Light++ under Extensions.

Education

I completed my BSc in Mathematics from the University of Sussex in 2016, and my MSc in Applied Mathematics from Imperial College in 2018.

For my master's project I wrote a coarse-grained simulation of tumour growth in C++ that targeted Imperial's high performance computing clusters. Source can be found here.
